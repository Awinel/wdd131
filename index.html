Chile

Chile is a long, narrow South American country known for its diverse landscapes, stretching over 2,700 miles along the western coast, from the arid Atacama Desert in the north to the glacial fjords of Patagonia in the south. It's a country of contrasts, boasting both the driest desert on Earth and glaciers, with a vibrant culture shaped by its unique geography.

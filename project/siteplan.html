Site Name

benjaminhuertadev.com

Benjamin Antonio Huerta Torres - Portfolio

This name represents my personal brand and portfolio website, it includes my name and last name with the dev in the end to showcase my development work.

Logo

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase my work as a web developer, including projects, skills, and contact information. It serves as a portfolio to demonstrate my abilities and attract potential clients or employers.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A potential employer visits the site to review my portfolio and skills.
  • Scenario 2: A client looking for web development services finds my site and wants to contact me for a project.
  • Scenario 3: A fellow developer visits my site to see examples of my work and possibly collaborate on future projects.
